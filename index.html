AN NFT PROJECT ON CARDANO
"Numismatis" is the ancient Latin word for "coins", and nowadays, its derivation sigifies the collection of coins. Among all kind of coins, commemorative coins are no normal, because it is a remark to remember the history of civilisation.
On its foundation, Carano is not built only with science and advanced technologies, but also with symbols, commemoration, and with the true care for humankind.
Having the same spirit, we desire to contribute tot he memory of the development of Cardano with the minting commemorative NFT coins by a vivid way, through 3D and AR technology.
